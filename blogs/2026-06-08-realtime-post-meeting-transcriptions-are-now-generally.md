RealtimeKit lets you build products where people meet over live audio and video — such as HealthTech, EdTech, proctoring, and other real-time platforms — on Cloudflare's global WebRTC infrastructure . Post-meeting transcription is now Generally Available, so completed RealtimeKit meetings can automatically produce full transcript files after they end. Those transcripts can also power AI-generated summaries for meeting notes, review workflows, and follow-up tasks after the transcript is available.
