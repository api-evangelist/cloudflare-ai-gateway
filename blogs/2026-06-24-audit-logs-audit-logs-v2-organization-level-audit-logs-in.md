You can now, as an Organization Super Administrator, view organization-level audit logs in the Cloudflare dashboard, in addition to the existing API access . Organization audit logs help you monitor activity across your organization. You can see who performed an action, what changed, when it happened, how it was performed, and whether it succeeded or failed.
