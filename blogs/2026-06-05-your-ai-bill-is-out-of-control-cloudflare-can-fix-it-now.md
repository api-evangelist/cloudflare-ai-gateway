Cloudflare has launched spend controls for AI Gateway, allowing organizations to set dollar-based budgets and track AI costs by model, provider, or custom attributes. The company also announced a closed beta for identity-driven budgets integrated with Cloudflare Access, enabling per-user and per-team cost attribution and policies based on existing identity providers.
