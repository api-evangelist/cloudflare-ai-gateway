You can now register a Cloudflare One Virtual Appliance and generate its license key directly from the dashboard, without contacting your account team. On the Connectors page, select Add an appliance and choose Virtual appliance to register a virtual appliance and generate its authentication key. Use Regenerate authentication key from a virtual appliance connector's menu to rotate its key.
