Partnering with Moondream to bring their latest model @cf/moondream/moondream3.1-9B-A2B to Workers AI. Moondream 3.1 is a fast vision language model built on a mixture-of-experts architecture with 9B total parameters and 2B active, delivering frontier-level visual reasoning while retaining fast, cost-efficient inference. Moondream 3.1 is designed for real-world vision tasks, with a 32K token context window for handling complex queries and structured outputs.
